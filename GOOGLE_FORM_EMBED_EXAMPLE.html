Google Form Embed Example

How to get your Google Form embed code:

  1. Open your Google Form
  2. Click the "Send" button in the top-right corner
  3. Click the "</>" (Embed) tab
  4. Copy the HTML code provided
  5. Extract the src URL from the iframe code
  6. Replace YOUR_FORM_ID_HERE in this example with your actual form ID

For the AI Workshop project, update the src URL in assets/js/main.js instead of directly editing the HTML.
